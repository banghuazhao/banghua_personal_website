About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in aeronautics and astronautics engineering advised by professor Wenbin Yu at Purdue University. My research lies in multiscale modeling of honeycomb sandwich structure using mechanics of structure genome (MSG). I am also working on ANSYS Workbench-SwiftComp GUI by using ACT. Aside from my research, I am interested in computer science and technology. Feel free to contact me over email (banghua.zhao@gmail.com).
